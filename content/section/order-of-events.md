## 3:30 pm - Shuttle bus

For those opting for the shuttle bus from Daylesford township, please be ready at the pickup points by 3:30 pm for a prompt arrival at 4 pm. Please let us know via the [RSVP](https://withjoy.com/freyamichael/rsvp) page if you wish to use the shuttle bus.

## 4:00 pm - Welcome to The Burrow

Everyone arrives at The Burrow in Denver, on the outskirts of Daylesford. **Head on over to the [travel](/travel) page for more information.**

## 4:30 pm - Ceremony

Delight in our nervousness as we get married in the forest only a short walk from the reception venue.

## 5:00 pm - Reception

While Freya and Michael will disappear for photos, grab a drink and have a nibble from the grazing board while you make new friends.

## 6:00 pm - Food

Sit down and enjoy pizzas underneath the festoon lights in The Paddock. If you have dietary requirements, please head over to the pizza truck.

## 8:30 pm - Dancing

Head over to The Barn for dancing and good tunes.

## 11:00 pm - Head on home

For those who have taken the shuttle bus, please rejoin for your journey back to town.
